Framegen
-
Controls and settings live inside the player: hover a video, the FG and ⚙ buttons appear on the left.

Two GPUs (laptop)

Chrome on Windows often lands on the integrated GPU - fps barely improves.

  1. Windows Settings → System → Display → Graphics
  2. Add Chrome → High performance
  3. Fully restart Chrome

Check: the GPU line on the Status tab should show the discrete card.

Requirements

Recent desktop Chrome/Edge and a GPU from roughly 2016 or newer. Firefox, Safari and phones - no.

How to use

Hover any video → the FG (on/off) and ⚙ (settings) buttons appear on the left. Settings include Compare - an original/processed split slider.

What does not work

Netflix, Crunchyroll and other DRM sites - the browser never hands us their pixels, a platform limitation. On some sites the video may reload for a couple of seconds the first time you enable FG (CORS) - that is normal.
